About Me

I have been a passionate make up artist for 10 years now and I specialize in Bridal make up and artistry. It is a rare privilege to help beautiful brides look their best on their special day amd perfecting the art of beauty. I don't see this just as a job but also my own way of empowering ladies out there and adding a touch of magic to their lovely faces as they say “yes” to the man of their dreams on the most important day of their live.
